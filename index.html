Welcome to Bethesda Game Studios

Bethesda Game Studios is an American video game developer and a division of Bethesda Softworks based in Rockville, Maryland. The company was established in 2001 as the spin-off of Bethesda Softworks' development unit, with the original staff joining together to create Bethesda Game Studios.
